VANGUARD SERVICES

Vanguard Citizen Services (VCS) is a creative studio that acts as the administrative heart of Fort Toward, the last free zone in the land of Soji. Their primary goal is to achieve total sector stability by providing a safe harbor for all citizens, while transforming Fort Toward from a military stronghold into a thriving home where progress is protected and resources from The Reserves are shared equitably.
