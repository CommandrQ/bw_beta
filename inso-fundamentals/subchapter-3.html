THREADART APPLICATION

A Threadart is the final expression of Inso. Once Vi, Ki, and Chi are aligned, the current can be shaped into these functions:

OFFENSIVE (Force)

Directs focus outward to disrupt or displace external energy worldlines.

DEFENSIVE (Resistance)

Reinforces your presence to absorb pressure without losing alignment.

UTILITY (Enhancement)

Modifies movement or perception for better environmental interaction.
