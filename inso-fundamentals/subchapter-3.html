THREADART APPLICATION

A Threadart is the deliberate expression of unified intention. When the Triad of Energies is aligned, the resulting Inso current can be shaped into categorized functions to interact with the worldline.

OFFENSIVE THREADARTS FORCE

Techniques that direct the stabilization current outward to displace or disrupt external forces. These require peak Ki (Body) and sharp Vi (Mind) focus.

DEFENSIVE THREADARTS RESISTANCE

Techniques used to reinforce protection or resistance. These rely heavily on Chi (Soul) to maintain internal direction and stability while absorbing pressure.

UTILITY THREADARTS ENHANCEMENT

Techniques that enhance movement, perception, or environmental interaction. This requires the most precise balance of all three energies to sustain long-term effects.

Measured Implementation

Mastery of Threadarts is not merely about power, but about choosing the right application at the right time. An aligned individual uses focus to think before reacting, choosing long-term truth over short-term impulse.
