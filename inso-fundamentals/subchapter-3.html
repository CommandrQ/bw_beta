THE PRACTICE OF THREADARTS

Have you ever noticed how some people can walk into a room and immediately settle a conflict? Or how others seem to move with an invisible shield against negativity?

That is the practice of Inso. When you align your Mind, Body, and Soul, you are no longer just reacting—you are weaving your own reality.

🛡️ Defensive Application

In real life, this is Boundary Setting. When someone is chaotic or loud, you use your Chi (Soul) to remember who you are, preventing their storm from entering your ship.

⚔️ Offensive Application

This is Decisive Action. When a truth needs to be spoken or a task needs to be done, you use Ki (Body) to move with total intention, making your presence felt without being aggressive.

🌀 Utility Application

This is Present Focus. You use your Vi (Mind) to cut through brain fog and distractions, allowing you to learn and observe at a much higher level than those who are "adrift."

By practicing these simple alignments, you gain the confidence to step further into Vanguard University. You aren't just a student anymore; you are becoming a practitioner.
