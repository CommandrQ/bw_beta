LOCAMET LEGACY // MODULE 02

THE TRIAD OF ENERGIES

The Knights of Locamet believed that a human being is like a ship. To sail through the storms of time, you need more than just a strong hull; you need a clear eye and a steady hand.

They simplified our internal world into three pillars. Without balance in these three, we become "adrift"—easily influenced by the chaos of others.

✦ VI (The Mind)

Vi is your Watchtower. It is your ability to pause, look at a situation, and see the truth behind the noise.

Real Life: It’s the difference between reacting in anger and choosing to respond with calm judgment. It is vital because without it, you are easily manipulated by distractions.

✦ KI (The Body)

Ki is your Vessel. It is your physical presence, your health, and the energy you bring into a room. It is the bridge between thinking and doing.

Real Life: It’s your physical boundaries and your stamina. It is vital because a brilliant mind (Vi) is useless if the body is too exhausted or weak to take action.

✦ CHI (The Soul)

Chi is your Compass. It is your sense of "Who am I?" and your emotional core. It keeps your direction steady when the path gets dark.

Real Life: It’s your integrity and emotional resilience. It is vital because without a strong sense of self, you will eventually tire of doing the right thing and lose your way.
