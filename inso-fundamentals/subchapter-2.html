LOCAMET LEGACY // MODULE 02

THE TRIAD OF ENERGIES

The Knights of Locamet believed that a human being is like a ship. To sail through the storms of life, you need a clear eye and a calm hand.

✦ VI (The Mind)

Your Watchtower. The ability to pause and see the truth behind the fog.

Real Life: Choosing calm judgment over a gut reaction. Without it, you're easily manipulated by distractions.

✦ KI (The Body)

Your Vessel. Your physical presence and health are the bridge between thinking and doing.

Real Life: Your stamina and boundaries. A clear mind needs a strong ship to actually take action.

✦ CHI (The Soul)

Your Compass. Your integrity and sense of self that keeps you on path in the dark.

Real Life: Your emotional resilience. Without it, you lose your way when things get tough
