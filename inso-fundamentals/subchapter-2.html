MODULE II

THE TRIAD OF ENERGIES

Alignment is not a passive event; it is a rigorous practice. To master Inso, you must refine the three internal pillars of your existence.

✦ VI: THE MIND

Vi governs your thought, focus, and perception. In a chaotic worldline, a strong Vi reduces mental confusion and allows for upright judgment.

  • Posture: Maintain an upright spine to facilitate clear mental currents.
  • Breath: Use controlled inhalation to anchor your focus.
  • Isolation: Practice removing environmental distractions to strengthen internal reasoning.
✦ KI: THE BODY

Ki governs your physical presence, movement, and endurance. It translates your internal intention into physical reality.

  • Discipline: Engage in repetitive, structured physical forms to build resilience.
  • Balance: Master your center of gravity to ensure intention is never displaced.
  • Coordination: Synchronize your physical movement with the cadence of your breath.
✦ CHI: THE SOUL

Chi governs your emotional balance, memory, and identity. It provides the direction and meaning behind every Threadart.

  • Resonance: Practice deep breathing into the chest to stabilize emotional spikes.
  • Reflection: Review your past experiences without judgment to solidify your identity.
  • Equanimity: Maintain a calm core even when Vi and Ki are under immense pressure.
