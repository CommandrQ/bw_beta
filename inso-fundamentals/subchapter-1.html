THE ESSENCE OF INSO

Recruit, your journey begins with understanding the nature of the power you seek to shape. Inso is not a tool you pick up; it is a state you inhabit.

"Inso is defined as the stabilizing current of Solaryn within the structure of Time. It develops within an individual through consistent discipline, awareness, and balance."

The Internal Source

Unlike common sorceries that pull from the air or the earth, Inso is strictly an internal development. It cannot be granted by another, nor can it be found in a relic. It is the result of your own measured implementation of focus.

The Triad Alignment

Inso forms specifically when three distinct energies of your being align under a unified intention. If one is out of balance, the current flickers and fails.

VI Mind
KI Body
CHI Soul

In the following subchapters, we will break down the specific training methods for these energies to prepare you for the shaping of Threadarts.
